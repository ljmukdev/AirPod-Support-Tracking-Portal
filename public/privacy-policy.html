Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

LJM ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Warranty Registration

When you register a warranty, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Product information (serial number, security barcode)
  • Purchase information (eBay order number if applicable)

1.2 Website Usage

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Warranty Administration: To register, manage, and process warranty claims
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Product Improvement: To improve our products and services
  • Marketing: To send promotional communications (only if you opt-in)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Marketing Communications

If you opt-in to marketing communications:

  • You will receive infrequent emails about new services, promotions, and special offers
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails
  • Your data will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Opting out does not affect warranty coverage or support

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in warranty administration (under strict confidentiality)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent for marketing

To exercise these rights, contact us at airpodsupport@ljmuk.co.uk

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • For the duration of your warranty period
  • As required by law (typically 7 years for financial records)
  • Until you request deletion (subject to legal requirements)

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Website functionality
  • Security

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests:

  • Email: airpodsupport@ljmuk.co.uk
  • Include "Privacy Request" in the subject line
