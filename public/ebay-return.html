Identifying Counterfeit AirPods

Apple manufactures each AirPod part (left earbud, right earbud, and case) with a unique serial number that is different for each component. If you find that all your AirPod parts have identical serial numbers, or if serial numbers are missing, this indicates the items are counterfeit. Counterfeit manufacturers replicate the serial number from the packaging box (which matches the case serial number) onto all parts, making them appear genuine when checked through Apple's online serial number identifier tool. However, authentic Apple AirPods will always have three distinct serial numbers - one for each earbud and one for the case.
