Troubleshooting

Before You Open Your Package

If you haven't opened your package yet and have questions about compatibility or the pairing process, please contact us before opening. Once the AirPod case is opened and the replacement is paired, items cannot be returned or exchanged unless a fault is present.

Contact: support@ljm.com

AirPods won't connect

Try these steps:

  1. Make sure your iPhone or iPad is updated to the latest iOS/iPadOS version
  2. Check that Bluetooth is enabled in Settings
  3. Forget your AirPods in Settings > Bluetooth, then try pairing again
  4. Reset your AirPods by holding the setup button for 15 seconds until the light flashes amber, then white
  5. Restart your iPhone or iPad and try again
  6. Make sure both AirPods are in the case and the case lid is open
Only one AirPod works

Solutions:

  1. Check that both AirPods are properly seated in the charging case
  2. Ensure both AirPods are charged (place them in the case for at least 15 minutes)
  3. Go to Settings > Bluetooth > AirPods and check that both AirPods are detected
  4. Try resetting your AirPods by holding the setup button for 15 seconds
  5. Clean the AirPods and charging contacts with a soft, dry cloth
  6. If the issue persists, the replacement AirPod may need to be re-paired separately
Case won't charge

Check these:

  1. Use a different charging cable and power adapter
  2. Try a different power source (wall outlet instead of computer USB)
  3. Check that the charging port is clean and free of debris
  4. Ensure the charging cable is fully inserted
  5. Wait at least 10-15 minutes and check if the charging indicator appears
  6. If using wireless charging, make sure the case is properly positioned on the charger
  7. Check that your AirPods are properly seated in the case
No setup animation appears

Manual pairing steps:

  1. Make sure your device is unlocked and on the home screen
  2. Open the charging case lid near your device
  3. Press and hold the setup button on the back of the case for 15 seconds
  4. If the animation still doesn't appear, go to Settings > Bluetooth
  5. Look for your AirPods in the list of available devices
  6. Tap on your AirPods to connect manually
  7. If your AirPods don't appear, try forgetting them first (if they were previously paired) and then re-pair
AirPods connect but have no sound

Fix audio issues:

  1. Check your device's volume level
  2. Go to Settings > Bluetooth > AirPods and ensure the audio is set to AirPods
  3. Check that the AirPods are selected as the audio output in Control Center
  4. Clean the AirPods mesh with a soft, dry brush
  5. Try playing audio from a different app
  6. Reset your AirPods and re-pair them
Battery drains quickly

Battery optimization:

  1. Ensure both AirPods are fully charged before first use (leave in case for 30 minutes)
  2. Check battery status in Settings > Bluetooth > AirPods
  3. Make sure the charging case is fully charged
  4. Reset your AirPods if the battery indicator seems inaccurate
  5. If the replacement part is new, it may need a few charge cycles to calibrate

Still Need Help?

If none of these solutions work, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • Your security code from the package
  • Your device model (iPhone/iPad) and iOS version
  • A description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Steps you've already tried

Email: support@ljm.com
